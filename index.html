Critical Questions

Does this "read my mind"?

No. This is an EMG (Electromyography) device, not EEG. It reads electrical signals from your muscles (Masseter & Suprahyoid), not your brain. It only detects words you intentionally try to say.

Why use Arduino Nano 33 BLE Sense?

This specific board is the gold standard for TinyML. It has a powerful nRF52840 processor (64MHz) capable of running TensorFlow Lite models in real-time and supports Bluetooth Low Energy for the mobile app connection.

How accurate is the device?

With proper electrode placement and training (approx. 50 samples per word), simple vocabularies (e.g., "Water", "Pain", "Yes", "No") can achieve 85-95% accuracy. Background noise and electrode shift are the main challenges.
